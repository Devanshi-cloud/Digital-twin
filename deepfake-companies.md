### How These Companies Fit the B2C Deep-Fake Landscape  
All six firms deliver synthetic-media products that end-users can access directly-either via a web portal, mobile app, or API that powers consumer-grade avatar/video creation. Their offerings range from simple face-swap utilities (The Indian Deepfaker) to sophisticated multi-language AI avatars (TrueFan AI, GAN.AI, Unscript AI) and immersive holographic avatars (Ikonz). Each complies, at least publicly, with India’s 2022 amendment to the IT Rules that requires clear labeling, persistent metadata, and user consent for AI-generated content.
